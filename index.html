About Us

Taste of Freedom serves up American history with a delicious twist! Our homemade premium ice cream features flavors inspired by iconic traditions and holidays. We're scooping up smiles and sweet memories!

Ol' Faithful

Experience the timeless taste of our "Ol' Faithful" vanilla bean ice cream. Made with pure vanilla bean flecks, this flavor is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser, just like the iconic geyser.

Cookies n' Cream

A classic flavor that combines creamy vanilla ice cream with chunks of chocolate sandwich cookies.

Founding Father's Fudge

Rich chocolate ice cream bursting with decadent fudge swirls and chunks of chewy brownies. This flavor is a true celebration of chocolate decadence, perfect for chocoholics and anyone who loves a classic flavor combination.
